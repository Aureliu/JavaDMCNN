Jet Part of Speech Tags

For most tasks, Jet uses a small part-of-speech inventory.  In this scheme, proper names and a few other words (such as relative pronouns) are not assigned a part-of-speech tag.

n
 noun (normally accompanied by number=singular or number=plural feature)
v
 base form of verb
tv
 tensed form of verb (normally accompanied by number=singular or number=plural feature)
ving
 verb present participle
ven
 verb past participle
adj
 adjective (including comparative and superlative)
adv
 adverb
pro
 pronoun
det
 determiner
p
 preposition
dp
 adverbial particle
w
 modal
q
 quantifier
cconj
 coordinate conjunction
sconj
 subordinating conjunction
